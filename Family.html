ROZALI BIN ISMAIL

{ Father }

A name called Abah. Abah is a strict but loving person. Abah takes care of us siblings well and responsibly. In silence, Abah often gives admonitions as a sign of love with words that sometimes sound quite harsh and in an angry tone but not really because that is how Abah shows his love and affection to the children. Dad also always backs us up when we get angry with mom. Abah has lightened our family's burden a lot.

NOR ASMAH BINTI OTHMAN

{ Mother }

A name called Umi. Umi is a motherly soul and her words easily touch the corners of the heart. Mother strengthens our brothers and sisters. A mother's advice is the most useful because she is the one who best understands the nature of her children. Umi knows what we like and what we don't like. Sometimes Umi is angry, not hate, but there is a lesson to be learned.

NUR ADLEEN IMAN BINTI ROZALI

{ Sister }

The name called Kak Long. Her aura makes other siblings respect her. A tough person, sometimes stubborn as well but quick to soften. The sister who knows how to keep all the secrets of her siblings and her siblings acts as our mother in giving advice. Sometimes there is a bossy attitude and we are quite annoying. But if there is no sister's magpie mouth at home, then our house is quiet..

MUHAMMAD FADHIL BIN ROZALI

{ BROTHER }

Nickname Abe Ngoh. It seems selfish because he doesn't take much notice of siblings' affairs, but when there is a problem, he is the place we look for. Once found, the problem can be solved. Abe Ngoh quietly takes care of his heart and protects his younger siblings well and full of responsibility..
